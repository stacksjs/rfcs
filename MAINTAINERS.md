Maintainers merge editorial and compatible changes, sponsor proposals, record
decisions, and run ratification votes. A maintainer must disclose material
employment, financial, or project conflicts on an RFC before voting.

## Membership

A new maintainer requires:

1. at least two substantive RFC reviews or one successfully shepherded RFC;
2. a public nomination issue open for at least 14 days;
3. approval by two-thirds of non-conflicted maintainers; and
4. an update to this file in the same decision commit.

Until there are three maintainers, every maintainer appointment and every
breaking or ratification decision additionally requires a 30-day public review
and an explicit response to each unresolved blocking objection.

A maintainer may resign by pull request. Removal for inactivity, abuse, repeated
non-disclosure, or loss of project trust uses the appeal procedure in
[GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md) and requires a two-thirds vote excluding the
subject.
